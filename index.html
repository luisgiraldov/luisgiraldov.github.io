October 2018

This is the place that I have created for the people who are potential clients or for people that are interested about what kind of work I have done, and what knowledge I have on the Web Development field.

Research and Discovery

First of all, I looked through different websites seeking for that inspirational touch that other websites don't have, and combine it with designs patterns that were trending at the time.

I had to make some choices, either using frameworks or build everything from scratch, since the main idea is to show off what abilities a web developer should possess. Afterward, I decided to mix the complexity of things from scratch and the simplicity and speed of a framework.

Wireframing and Sketch

Generally, I like to start every project with sketches made by hand which helps me to focus on the functionality and user experience that I want to achieve, instead of focusing on the visual aspect of the site. Once I got that done I was able to focus on how it was going to look. Also, I really had fun drawing all over the paper smashing a brainstorm on that piece of freedom.

Finally, I picked the best solution of each section from every sketch I had made, and I implemented it on Adobe XD, to give it a professional appearance.

Website Design

The last stage was the design, in this part I wanted to show something kind of fancy but simple at the same time, not too crazy but not boring either, the purpose is when the user sees the website get impressed by the first look and as she or he scrolls down the page will get a calmer experience, having a softened color to make the reading easier. Also, I wanted the user to have a smooth scrolling experience, when navigating through the website. In conclusion what I did was to take what I like the most, like colorful, fun, a touch of fancy and professionalism, and combine them, focusing on how to give the best user experience. As a result, it was a combination of emotions a lot of fun and hard work, all of that printed on this screen.
